History

Founded in 1987 by friends Jake Henderson and Sarah Thompson, White Water Rafting emerged from a shared passion for adventure and nature. Starting with a few rafts and a team of dedicated guides, they pioneered thrilling expeditions through the untamed rivers.

Their commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship quickly set White Water Rafting apart. Over the years, they expanded their fleet and route offerings while maintaining a focus on preserving the pristine wilderness they explored.
